About The Hymns Book

A labor of love for worshippers everywhere

🎵

Our Mission

The Hymns Book is a free, open platform designed to help worshippers, musicians, and churches access beautiful hymns and worship songs with accurate guitar chords. We believe music is a powerful way to connect with faith, and everyone should have easy access to the songs that inspire them.

🔍

Find any song instantly with our powerful search and filter system.

📄

Sheet Support

Upload and view PDF, PNG, and JPG hymn sheets alongside lyrics.

How to Use

  1. Browse — Explore songs by category or use the search bar
  2. View — Click any song to see lyrics with chords aligned above
  3. Transpose — Use + / − buttons to change the key
  4. Save — Click the heart to add songs to your favorites
  5. Contribute — Add new songs using the "Add Song" page
